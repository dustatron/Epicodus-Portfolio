About Me

I am always looking for ways to infuse creativity in everything I do.

Breif History

I grew up in and around Olympia Washington. I went to Evergreen State College and moved to Portland to follow a job in the medical supply industry. One day at the end of summer I was struck from behind by a CITY BUS on SW 5th and Burnside while riding my bike. That near death experience inspired me to quit my job and pursue a career in the film industry. Despite my lack of experience and industry contacts I managed to get my foot in the door as a teleprompter and then as a Production Assistant on late night, long form infomercials.

That was 17 years ago now. I used my love of all things computers to maneuver myself in to the position of onset data tech. Managing large amounts of footage and assuring image fidelity. From there I worked as a video editor and and motion graphics animator. While I still love the film industry I am ready for new challenges and I am interested in working with different teams of people.
