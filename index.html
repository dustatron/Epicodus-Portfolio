About Me

I am always looking for ways to infuse creativity in everything I do.

Breif History

As a kid I was a big movie nerd when I was a kid. In my 20s I purchased a real video camera and found a community of film nerds like myself and we made short movies every month for years. It was through this community I got my first film production job on a long form infomercial. I emptied the garbage and got people coffee. From there I moved into Teleprompting. I taught myself how to edit and animate and was able to conceive my contacts to trust me with delivering their product from a pile of footage to a final product.

I am now trying to leverage my ability to self teach and master new skills to move into a career as a web developer. Film production is an interesting field but it requires a large time commitment for every project. My goal is to have a career that challenges me to keep learning and to stay agile like video editing but allows me to work less than 14 hours a day and schedule a day off and actually take that day off.
